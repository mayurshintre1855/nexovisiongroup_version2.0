What is GST cash credit?+

It is a working-capital facility that may use factors such as GST filing history and business performance while assessing eligibility. Actual terms depend on the lending partner and your profile.

Who can apply for business funding?+

Eligibility varies by product. Established businesses with regular financial records and GST filings are generally better placed for assessment.

How much funding can I receive?+

The amount depends on turnover, business vintage, repayment capacity and the lender's evaluation. Our eligibility tool gives only an indicative range.

How long does the process take?+

Timing depends on documentation and the chosen product. We help keep every stage organised so the process can move efficiently.
